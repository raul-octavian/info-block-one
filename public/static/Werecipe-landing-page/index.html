Mission

Our mission is to deliver the best and the only app a home cook will need. We believe in the power of community, and that food tastes better with friends. Trust Werecipe to store your recipes, connect you with friends, and save your family cooking secrets.

Search

Explore international cuisines, and get access to more than 2.3 mil recipes with a simple search.

Save

Easily save your recipes to your cookbooks through URL import, manual input, or from a scanned recipe book. Save your recipes in private, family, or public cookbooks.

Own

Make each recipe your own, create new versions of the same recipe, make sure that the recipe screams YOU.

Cook

Cook the best food you can cook. Enjoy food as intended, but remember to make it your OWN.

Share

Join large public communities, or make your own, discuss with your family or just help others. Whatever your motivation remember to share your creations and recipes with FAMILY and FRIENDS. Make your voice heard and remember that food tasted better with friends.

Repeat
